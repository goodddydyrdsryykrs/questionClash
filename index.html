How to Play Question Clash! 🎮
1

Answer Questions — Choose your Grade Level or ISD Path D (Algebra 1). Answer 3 correct to earn energy and enter the Lab!

2

The Lab — Your home base. Walk into glowing circles to teleport: 🏪 Shop, 🌍 World Map, ⚒️ Craft, 🎓 Class. Leaderboard trophies line the far wall.

3

Earn Currencies — 🪙 Silver from killing mobs · 💎 Clash Cash from challenge questions & shop · 🧱 Scrap from lab turns · ⭐ XP from everything!

4

Level Up — Earn XP to reach Level 5. Each level unlocks more power. Your XP bar shows at the top of every screen.

5

Challenge Questions — 15% chance of a ⚡ Challenge Question worth +50 Clash Cash! These are harder but worth it.

⚔️

Battle Arena — Fight waves of mobs (Ogres, Goblins, Trolls, Skeletons, Dark Mages, Fire Drakes). Kills drop Silver & materials. Press R to switch between Sword and Bow. Each wave gets harder. First to clear all mobs wins the wave!

⛏️

The Mine — Hold-click blocks to mine them. Ores in the floor AND ceiling. Find Coal, Iron, Gold, Diamond, Mystery blocks. Cave gets cleared = +500 XP bonus! Higher pickaxe tier = faster mining.

🌲

The Forest — Click trees to chop them. 5 tree types: Oak, Pine, Birch, Magic (purple glow!), Ancient (huge roots). Chop ALL trees = "Great Job!! +500 XP!!" Trees shake and darken as HP drops, then fall with animation leaving a stump.

🎯

Archery Range — Right-click to shoot arrows (gravity applies!). 6 targets at different distances: Close (1pt), Medium (3pt), Far (5pt), Bullseye (10pt!). 10 arrows per round. Hit all targets = +200 XP bonus!

🌐

Multiplayer Servers — Join or create a live server to play with classmates! Massive chunk-based world with caves, ores, trees, and roaming mobs. Build structures, mine resources, fight monsters together. Press Tab for player list, T to chat, 1-9 to switch hotbar slots.

🔧

Crafting Tools — Walk to the ⚒️ circle in the Lab. You need materials first! Get Iron from the Mine, Bone from killing Skeletons, Wood from the Forest.

📊

10 Tiers × 5 Levels = 50 Levels per tool! Each tier gives a completely new look and massive stat boost. Earn XP by using each tool to level it up.

Tool Tiers:
🪵 Tier 1: Wooden (1×)🪨 Tier 2: Stone (1.4×) ⚙️ Tier 3: Iron (1.9×)🟡 Tier 4: Gold (2.5×) 💎 Tier 5: Diamond (3.2×)⬛ Tier 6: Netherite (4.2×) 🔮 Tier 7: Crystal (5.5×)🔥 Tier 8: Infernal (7×) ✨ Tier 9: Celestial (9×)👑 Tier 10: GODLIKE (12×)
🛡️

Ultimate Armor Kit — Craft the legendary armor when ALL tools reach Level 5+ AND you have 20 iron, 10 gems, 15 gold, 10 bone, 8 coal, 5 wood. Gives permanent -40% damage from all enemies!

⚔️

3D PvP Arena — Click "PvP Battle" on the title screen. One player creates a room and shares the code. Both players drop into a dungeon and fight with bows! Right-click to shoot. First to 5 kills wins a round. Best of 3 rounds wins the match!

🔥

Direct Duel — Click "Duel" for a quick 1v1 question battle. Answer questions faster than your opponent. First to 5 correct answers wins! Scores update live every 0.8 seconds.

🌍

World PvP Challenge — Inside ANY world, tap ⚔️ Challenge a Friend at the bottom. Creates a real-time race in that world's activity:
• ⚔️ Arena = who kills the most mobs in 90s
• ⛏️ Mine = most blocks broken in 60s
• 🌲 Forest = most trees chopped in 60s
• 🎯 Archery = highest score in 60s

🌐

Multiplayer Servers PvP — In a server, reach Tier 2 on any tool to unlock PvP against other players. Then you can attack each other with swords and bows in the open world!

🏪

The Shop — Walk into the gold circle in the Lab to enter the shop. Spend Clash Cash (💎) or Silver (🪙) on powerful upgrades!

Shop Categories:
⭐ XP Boosts — 500/1500/5000 XP packs (100-800 Clash Cash) ⚒️ Tool Upgrades — 2× XP for sword/pickaxe/axe (100-350 Clash Cash) 🪙 Silver Deals — Buy Silver with Clash Cash 📦 Material Packs — Iron, Gem, Gold bundles for crafting 💎 Clash Cash — Buy Clash Cash with Silver (1000 = 200 CC) 🏦 Trading Hall — Swap materials: Wood→Iron, Iron→Gems, Silver→Clash Cash
🎒

Mega Material Pack — Best value in the shop! 400 Clash Cash gets you everything needed for the Ultimate Armor Kit in one purchase.

🎒

Students — Click "My Class" on the title screen or walk into the 🎓 circle in the Lab. Enter your teacher's class code to join. Once joined, you'll see all your assignments!

📋

Assignments — Your teacher can assign specific subjects, grade levels, and number of questions. Click "Start" on any assignment to play it directly!

🎓

Teachers — Click "Teacher Login" on the login screen. Create an account with your school info. You get a random class code automatically — share it with your students! Your dashboard shows student progress, XP, waves completed, and activity status.

📊

Teacher Dashboard — 6 tabs: Overview (stats + class code), My Classes, Assignments (create/delete), Students (searchable list with live stats), Class Battle (challenge other classes!), Reports (top student, averages, totals).

🏆

Leaderboard — 6 categories: ⭐ XP, 🪙 Silver, ⚔️ Waves Defeated, ⛏️ Blocks Mined, 💀 Mobs Killed, 🌲 Trees Chopped. Press L while in any world to see a quick leaderboard overlay!
